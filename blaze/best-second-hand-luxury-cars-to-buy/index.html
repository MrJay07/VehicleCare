5 Best Second-hand Luxury Cars to Buy in India – VehicleCare

by Anisha Srivastava · Published May 12, 2022 · Updated May 19, 2022

If you’re looking to buy a luxury car but don’t want to pay the brand new price, you might be in luck. While used cars don’t have the same appeal as their new counterparts, there are some second-hand luxury cars that are of exceptional quality and can be purchased at a discount. Here are five of the best second-hand luxury cars available in India today, along with links to more information about each one and where to find them online.

Best second-hand luxury cars to buy in India

When it comes to buying a new car, you have a lot of options. But buying pre-owned is just as good an option, if not better. Many people don’t know that several European cars are available in their pre-owned form with warranty and at competitive prices. So if you’re looking to buy a used car, we have five great choices here. Check them out below!

 

BMW 7 Series

One of BMW’s most luxurious models, it has one of its largest vehicle ranges available with several variants, making it more affordable as a pre-owned car. Make sure to get fully checked by a mechanic before buying; models built between 2004 and 2010 are known to have problems with their brake discs. There is also an updated model available, but prices will be higher. It costs between Rs 15 lakh and Rs 20 lakh used.

 

Mercedes S Class

The Mercedes S Class is one of those models that immediately demands respect as soon as you step inside. It’s big, it’s luxurious and it will take you from A to B without breaking a sweat. And then there are all those extras that make it such an incredible car: GPS navigation, rear entertainment system, and heated seats to name but a few! If you can afford one and want something reliable and comfortable with bags of character then look no further than a used Mercedes S Class.

 

Audi A8

If you’re looking to own an Audi, but cannot afford one brand new, then it is time to look into buying a pre-owned model. The A8 from 2009 is one of the finest luxury sedans on offer and comes with the 4.2-liter V8 engine, Quattro all-wheel-drive system, and a 6-speed automatic transmission.

 

Land Rover Range Rover Sport

If you’re looking to drive around in comfort, safety, and style on both Indian and foreign roads, it’s hard to beat a Land Rover. While prices may vary depending on demand and other factors, it’s possible to pick up a 2013 Range Rover Sport at below Rs 100 lakh. At that price, you get one of the most powerful engines among its competitors (the legendary 5.0L V8), as well as an extremely comfortable interior that can seat up to 7 people comfortably.

 

Jaguar XJL Supercharged

This classic car is not only good-looking but also a perfect blend of speed and style. It has elegant interiors with intuitive design and premium comfort with front sporty seating arrangement and 7 seat option. In spite of its bigger size, it is faster than any other sports car in its segment due to a powerful V8 engine that delivers 542bhp. The auto variant gets an 8-speed transmission system with paddle-shift controls that helps you to accelerate faster. The car enjoys a top speed of 300 kmph.

 

 

Best second hand luxury car under 5 lakhs

The Audi A4, Mercedes Benz C Class, and BMW 3 Series are some excellent second-hand options. If you’re looking to buy a certified pre-owned luxury car then stick to models from brands like Mercedes and BMW, which have been producing new cars for decades and have kept their old models up to date with changes so that there is no compromise on safety or quality. When you buy a used car, it pays to know about maintenance, common issues, and parts so that you can check out cars carefully before making your purchase. Here are some important tips you should keep in mind when buying an old model

 

Best-used luxury SUV to buy in India

The Range Rover has a legendarily luxurious interior and rich history, and as long as you don’t need all that space on a daily basis, it’s an affordable option. This particular model is available in Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, and Kolkata.

 

Most reliable used luxury cars in India

Buying a used car is never an easy task. There are many things to consider before buying a used car, such as quality, maintenance cost, and safety. If you’re looking to buy a pre-owned Mercedes or BMW, you need to be extra careful.

 

Cheapest second-hand luxury cars in India

A good way to save money on a new car is to look at second-hand options. While some may find it unappealing to buy a used car, it’s an incredibly economical option and can offer fantastic savings – around 50 percent or more off new-car prices. There are many benefits to buying a used vehicle, including lower costs, a wider range of choices, and low maintenance costs once you have bought.
